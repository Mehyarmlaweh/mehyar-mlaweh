I’m Mehyar Mlaweh, a PhD Candidate at LAMSADE Laboratory (Université Paris‑Dauphine – PSL), supervised by Prof. Tristan Cazenave. My thesis focuses on Monte Carlo Tree Search for Molecule Design, combining computational chemistry and AI for next‑generation drug discovery.

I blend research rigor with engineering creativity across deep learning, molecular modeling, and symbolic reasoning. Outside the lab, I’m a paper reader, coffee lover, music enthusiast, and proud Formula 1 fan.
